Scenario C insight — the most interesting case: The hire timestamp is the hard line. Three vendors booked before the Plnr joined — their dial commission splits 50/50 between EvntCue and the Plnr. Five vendors booked after — Plnr keeps everything. EvntCue earns $1,581 total on this event vs $625 in Scenario A — the pre-hire split is the mechanism that rewards early platform adoption without punishing the Plnr. The Orgnz never sees any commission flows. Their invoice shows vendor costs + Plnr service fee. That's it.
